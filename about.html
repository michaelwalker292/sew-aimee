About me.

I'm a busy mum of 3. I also work in school as a librarian. I love creating bespoke gifts for all occasions and am very happy to work with customers to create something personal and unique. I especially enjoy combining my love of books with sewing!

I've always enjoyed sewing, knitting and crafting but never considered doing it professionally until a few friends asked me to make them a cushion that I had made for my caravan. It crossed my mind that I could do this for a living but I dismissed it again! Until... last summer I made my Godson a memory blanket out of his babygros. Everyone loved it! I was so pleased with all the feedback that I thought 'why not?'
